Daiken's approach: high concentration in every single gummy + a premium oil form your body actually absorbs.

Compliance — Important for Video Approval

Safe language:

✓ "Supports brain development and vision"
✓ "Helps support healthy growth"
✓ "Designed for growing brains and eyes"
✓ "Nutritional support for picky eaters"

Do NOT say (will require re-edit or post-production cut):

✗ "This will cure..." or "This treats..."
✗ "Clinically proven to make kids smarter"
✗ "Your child NEEDS this" or any fear-based urgency
✗ Any specific disease or medical condition claims
✗ Any mention of competitor brand names

If your video contains medical/health cure claims, we will request a re-edit or cut in post-production. Use "supports" and "helps with" — never "cures," "treats," or "prevents."

DO's

✓Be authentic, warm — parent-to-parent energy
✓Show product clearly (box, blister packs, gummy close-up)
✓Do the smell test on camera — no fishy smell is key
✓Lead with concentration: 300mg per ONE gummy
✓Explain rTG absorption in simple terms
✓Show packaging as-is including Chinese text
✓Child must appear in video REQUIRED
✓Mention brand story (since 1961, quality awards)
✓Film in natural light, home setting
✓End with clear call to action

DON'Ts

✗Do NOT mention competitor brand names
✗Do NOT make medical claims ("cures," "treats," "prevents")
✗Do NOT rush — let viewers see the product
✗Do NOT use heavy background music
✗Do NOT over-explain science — 1-2 simple sentences for rTG
✗Do NOT hide bilingual packaging
✗Do NOT script word-for-word — keep it natural
✗Do NOT call it "Soft Chews" — say "gummies"
