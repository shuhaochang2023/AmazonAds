Give your honest verdict as the label-reading, ingredient-checking parent. Share what impressed you most — high omega-3 concentration? No sugar? No fishy taste? The convenient packaging?

DO's
+Be yourself — authentic, warm, parent-to-parent energy
+Show the product clearly (box, blister packs, individual gummy close-up)
+Do the smell test on camera — no fishy smell is a key differentiator
+Mention at least 3 key product benefits naturally
+Include your child's reaction if possible
+Film in natural light, home setting (kitchen, living room)
+End with a clear call to action
DON'Ts
xDo NOT show or mention competitor products
xDo NOT make medical claims ("this will cure..." or "this treats...")
xDo NOT rush — let viewers see and process the product
xDo NOT use heavy or distracting background music
xDo NOT use overly technical or scientific language
xDo NOT script it word-for-word — keep it natural and conversational
